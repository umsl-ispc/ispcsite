October 16, 7.00 pm – 8.30 pm in ESH 106
Topic: Geographic Information Systems
Presenter: Jeff Stahlhuth, Director, Information Technologies, Horner & Shifrin

Mr. Jeffrey Stahlhuth (PMP, MCSE, MCDBA, CCNA, RHCE, GISP) is Director, Information Technologies for Horner & Shifrin responsible for the establishment, planning, and administration of all H&S corporate information technology policies and goals.  He received a B.S. degree in Information Systems and a M.S. Information Systems from the University of Missouri, St. Louis and holds multiple certifications in Systems and Database Administration/Engineering.  Mr. Stahlhuth has over 15 years of experience in Geographic Information Systems and Information Systems and includes building Geographical Information Systems (GIS) applications, mobile GIS data collection, multiple-tier database applications, project management web sites, and customized ArcGIS applications from conception to rollout. His presentation will cover how Horner & Shifrin uses GIS for analysis and mobile data collection.
