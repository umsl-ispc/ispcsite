Facilities for IS Students

The MIS Lab (200 ESH): This lab is set up for IS students. You must swipe your ID card to gain access. You use your regular MyGateway ID and password to gain access to the machines.  The regular hours for the lab are: M-R: 7:30 am – midnight, F: 7:30 am – 11 pm, Sa:  7:30 am – 8 pm, and Su: 7:30 am – 10 pm. 

The Cybersecurity Lab (204 ESH): This lab is set up for IS students enrolled in the graduate and undergraduate certificates in cybersecurity and the undergraduate minor in cybersecurity.

IS Listservs: UMIS-L and GMIS-L that provide regular information about internships, jobs, IS user groups and activities.

Microsoft Software: Free Microsoft software including operating systems, developer tools, servers, and applications through Microsoft’s DreamSpark program.

Two students clubs: The IS Mentoring Club which focuses on developing soft skills such as resume writing and interviewing as well as forming mentor-protégé relationships between alumni and students.
The IS Programming Club focuses on developing technical skills through distinguished speakers, hackathons, game jams, competitions, and participation of women students in the Women’s Hackathon and the Grace Hopper Conference for Women Technologists.

The Information Systems Career Conference: An exclusive job fair for IS students.

UMSL’s Cybersecurity Conference – STLCyberCon: An opportunity for IS students to learn about the latest research on cybersecurity.

For more information, please contact: Dr. Dinesh Mirchandani ( 314-516-7354; mirchandanid@umsl.edu). Please follow the IS department on Twitter and Facebook
