JOB EXPERIENCE
I cannot emphasize how important it
is to list your experience in
chronological  order.  Recruiters and
their clients want to know what you
have done and WHEN!
If you have been at a company for a long time and
moved up the ranks, list each project/position with its
own time line.  This will emphasize your ability to
expand your skills and interests.
Do not go into long detail about what the company
does, detail what you did for the company.
List all your work experience.
