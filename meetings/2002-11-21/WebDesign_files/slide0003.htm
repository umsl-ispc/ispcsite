Why Some Web Designs Fail
lThe most common failure in web design is that there is not enough of a “discovery” phase before any work is done.  A lot of clients don’t know what is possible on the Web and rely on you to tell them how they can use the Web to enhance their day-to-day business.  Asking the right questions of your client and getting real answers is sometimes a tooth-pulling experience, but if you expect to provide a site that is a worthwhile business tool to your client it’s an imperative first step.
