Fact-Finding Questions
l What is the business objective of the website?
In other words – what do you want to use this
website for?  Designing a site just because an
executive told you to do it without telling you what
the site should achieve is not a good reason.  And
no, it is not an acceptable reason that “everyone
else is doing it”.  Of course these days you need a
website just to justify your company’s legitimacy,
so it is ok to make a brochure-style website with a
small amount of branding, directions to your
facilities, contact information, investor information,
etc. but it is probably not the best use of the Web
and it’s capabilities.
