Much of the Internet runs on free software such as Apache, BIND and Sendmail. Development centered around open source operating systems like Linux and FreeBSD has produced a lot of great software for networking, graphics, text processing and similar applications. However, many people aren't aware of the available tools or believe that they are too complicated to use. In this presentation, Mr. Wheeler will demonstrate and discuss programs that are of interest to those doing Web development, with particular emphasis on those tools that run on Windows, Mac OS X, and Unix platforms.
