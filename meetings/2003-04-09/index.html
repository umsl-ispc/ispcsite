Dave Schmitt, from Microsoft, gave an informative speech about Microsoft.Net. Dave provided an overview of the .Net framework, and its architecture. He also explained the capabilities of .Net and its individual components like VB.Net, ASP.Net etc. Finally he provided a brief comparison of .Net framework with Sun's J2EE(Java 2 Enterprize Edition).
