The purpose of the Information Systems Programming Club is to give students at UM-St. Louis real world examples of how technology is being applied in business applications, and to encourage and foster further understanding and knowledge of new developments in IS. These goals are accomplished through presentations and workshops on current topics by guest speakers from industry, site visits to leading software development companies, programming contests, and networking.

The ISPC has received a great deal of support from industry. We hope to expand and strengthen our cooperation with local businesses to give IS students at UM - St. Louis an opportunity to learn about exciting new technologies directly from the companies that develop them.
