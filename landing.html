Fall site update

The current site is an assortment of technologies, partially for compatibility due to large parts of the site being translated from older html. But a site update is in the works, and the entire site will be built from the ground up using everyone's favorite new-ish web framework AngularJS. Just as a preview we duct-taped some Angular onto the current site to show off its slick features. If you are curious the code for the new site is being updated weekly at the ISPC's Github account.

Thank you everyone for your help and participation in out first Career Conference and Job Fair at the University of Missouri – St. Louis this past April.
